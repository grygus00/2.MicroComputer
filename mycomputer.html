First love

To be honest, I do not remember the name of my first computer. I shared my first computer with my 8 years older brother, he was responsible to choose the computer and specifications. For me, it was too much because I was about 5 years old.
Firstly I fall in love with watching Dragon Ball anime that my brother borrowed from his friend on a floppy disk.
